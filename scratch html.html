About SpaceX

SpaceX is a private aerospace company founded by Elon Musk in 2002. They aim to reduce space transportation costs and enable Mars colonization.

Major Missions

  1. Falcon 1
    • First privately developed liquid-fueled rocket to reach orbit
  2. Falcon 9
    • Reusable rocket
    • Used to launch Starlink satellites
  3. Starship
    • Interplanetary transport system
    • In development for Mars missions
